Introduction to Evangelion

Neon Genesis Evangelion (EVA/NGE) is a Japanese mecha anime television series produced by Gainax and animated by Tatsunoko, directed by Hideaki Anno and broadcast on TV Tokyo from October 1995 to March 1996. (wiki)

Hideaki Anno, the director, is the Is the soul of the animation. The director incorporated many personal experiences into it, reflecting the confusion of young people in Japan at that time, which is actually a common phenomenon faced by young people all over the world.

Settings of this anime are mixed with a large number of religious elements, causing many Japanese anime imitation at the time.
